Notes
Three parts to the forest, two mazes at each end and the middle part that isn't a maze.

Heading in towards the middle of the forest, look for:
A thin animal trail leads further into the forest.
The trail begins to widen in this direction.

Heading out towards either edge of the forest, look for:
The forest appears to be thinning.
You can barely make out what appears to be the edge of this forest.
The path seems to lead to the edge of the trees.

If you get lost you only have to wander around until you can see them again.
