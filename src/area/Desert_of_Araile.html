Notes
* All things in Main Araille map are where things are located in relation to each other, the desert
  itself has a lot of rooms that lead into each other and is not fun to map. If you walk along the
  edge you can find everything except Nomad Village and the Temple, for those, just follow caravan 
  road. A few steps south from the gate you'll see a temple path, and keep following the road down 
  and west for the Village.
