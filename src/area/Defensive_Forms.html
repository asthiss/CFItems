Description

A reddish carapace covers this low-lying animal as it slowly moves across
your path.  You notice that it swings its head from side to side as if
trying to see everything that could come between it and its goal,
whatever that may be.  It sees you and pauses for a moment then continues
on its way, ignoring you.
