ABANDONED SIEGE ENCAMPMENT (2560)

Nestled against the mountains near Tir-Talath are the remains of a 
formidable siege encampment.  Once home to a clan of misguided lightwalkers, 
they were so blinded by their zealous crusade against a fantasy darkness 
that they did not see true evil approaching.  It would be their brutal 
downfall.

Long abandoned by its original inhabitants, the encampment is now home to an
imperfectly bound evil held at bay only by the vigilant forces of good in an 
epic stalemate.  But be wary, for only intrepid and high-ranked adventurers 
should pass beyond the protective palisade.
