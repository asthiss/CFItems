The Dragon Sea was written by Sattanos for exclusive use on

as you leave them alone.  The ships which sail the waves will
welcome those who ask, "Permission to come aboard?" and
hold a symbol the captain can recognize.

Many thanks to the patience and creativity of Vassagon who put
the wind in the sails of the ships.
