Mazes

FO - In a Forest (Maze1)
  To Green Hills : Look for "You can see some hills off this way."
  To Woody End   : Look for "You can see a clearing up ahead."

OF - The Old Forest (Maze2)
  To Woody End   : Look for "The huge hedge is up ahead."
  To Bombadil    : Look for "You can't tell if this is the way out..."

WB - Within the Barrows (Maze3)
  To Bombadil    : Look for "You can see the faint outline of a hill."
  To the wight   : Look for "The mists seem to continue on forever."

*first two mazes, (maze1,2) are each 9 rooms big, nonstatic maze, 
but using a few coins and a piece of paper quite easy to navigate.*
*going from hidden exit at X through the minimaze to old forest maze, fastest path is- s,e,s(last south is hidden)
down leads you to babblebrook, then forest maze(maze1)*
*Maze3 can be reached by going up near the entrance to Tom Bombadils(his name changed).
small maze, quite easy to navigate.*
*note: all mazes are cursed.*
