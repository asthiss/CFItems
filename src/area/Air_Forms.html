Air Forms

Area Map


    A magnificent bird flies before you, mostly covered in dark brown
    plumage, save for a bit of white at the base of the tail and
    golden-blond feathers at the back of its head.  Its bill and
    talons are a jet black in color.  A pair of keen eyes sweep
    the space before it, rapidly absorbing every detail, even so much
    as to be able to spot a small mouse from thousands of feet in
    the air. Its wings spread wide, to nearly seven feet, as it soars
    through the air.
