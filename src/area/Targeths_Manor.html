TARGETH TARGETHS MANOR 'TARGETHS MANOR'
After a lifetime of war, one of Thera's most experienced mercenaries decided to
find lands of his own to rule, rather than fight the wars of others.  Finding
fertile lands beyond the Shadow Peaks and with a personal army, the mercenary
Captain Targeth easily displaced the local Lord.  Though wary of the many
guards which now patrol the manor's lands, the daily life of the peasantry
remains mostly unchanged as they toil among the fields.  Lord Targeth himself
is said to be a firm master, though not unfair to his newfound populace.

Rebuilding the Manorhouse in a more defensible location, the serf village has
also seen improvements to better provide the basic necessities to the populace.
Travelers are warned to keep out of the fields at night as raiders from the
nearby Fhalaugash Keep are said to roam the darkness.
