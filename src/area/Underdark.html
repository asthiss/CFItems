* Scan doesn't work in the underdark except for races native to the underdark (drow, svirf, duergar) and caverndweller rangers.
* Transportation spells don't work.
* Roaming aggro mobs (cavefishers, dark-elf patrols).

H - Dark-elf house (key is hidden in a skeleton outside the door - once you attack one dark-elf, they're all aggro to you)
C - Cavefisher
R - Drider (spider axe)

G - Guardian
L - Kteng's Lab
