                                     Velkyn Oloth


Maze notes: It is broken up into 4 sectors, nw, sw, ne, se. If
you are in the ne sector, head all east till you hit the edge and go
south to get back to the path. If you are in the sw sector go all west
then north to the path and so on and so forth.

NOTES

Only a maze if you stray from the path. If you head to
Velkyn Oloth, use "Look" to find the right path.
