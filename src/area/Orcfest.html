Subject: Assemble the Horde!

Greetings, Orcs of Thera!  I wish to test your mettle, to see if any of you
young ones have what it takes to be a force within the Grinning Skull Village.
I have devised a test of sorts, that will require you to demonstrate cunning,
knowledge, skill and combat prowess.  This test will be open to all orcs who
are between the 20th and 30th ranks, and who have received at least some small
recognition from the Gods for their past (in other words, you need to have a
role that has received some Imm-granted XP).  The test will take place next
Saturday (January 26 at 1200 system time) as the Gods measure time, and will
commence at the Grinning Skull Village.

I am aware that orcs have many enemies, and invite those who wish to do combat
with the Horde as they travel through Thera doing my bidding, to assemble where
they will at this time as well.  I will ensure that there arise ample
opportunities for you to test the individuals in the Horde.
