Key:
= or " or // is a door or something that is barring your way (= is e/w, " is n/s, // is u/d).
This only applies to things that cannot be opened using the "open" command.
C You can crawl north to c, a one way trip
D You can crawl north to d, a one way trip 
L is for Lever
M is for Menhir
x You can crawl north to y, or south to z from here
y You can crawl south to x (Can crawl west when gnome sized)+
z You can crawl north to x (Can crawl west when gnome sized)+
W Gnome selling wands (invis).
S Gnome selling scrolls.
X Gnome selling maps.
If too large get the echo:
'You push with all your might, but still can't squeeze through the window'.
