----------------------------------------
This object, a beautiful platinum helm, can be referred to as 'platinum dragon helm'.
It has been imbued with a blessing.
Those with a balanced soul cannot use it.
People of a dark heart cannot use it.
It is worth 2500 copper, and is of 26th level of power.
When worn, it protects you against piercing for 8,
bashing for 8, slashing for 8 and magic for 0 points each.
When worn, it affects your ability to do damage by 1 points and your ability to land accurate blows by 1 points.

----------------------------------------
This object, a platinum blade, engraved with the sun, can be referred to as 'platinum blade sun'.
A magical aura surrounds it.
It has been imbued with a blessing.
Those with a balanced soul cannot use it.
People of a dark heart cannot use it.
Only an elf could put it to use.
It is worth 5000 copper, and is of 26th level of power.
It can cause 6d4 points of damage, at average 15.
When worn, it affects your ability to do damage by 5 points and your ability to land accurate blows by 5 points.
