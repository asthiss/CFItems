Keep of the Righteous

Level: 10 - 25 | Builder: Karithia |

Area Map

A granite stone lies here buried partially into the earth. Several
traveler runes cover the stone, leaving a message. The message reads:

Karithia created this area in 2002 for the Carrion Fields Mud. Use of this
area is restricted without written consent.
