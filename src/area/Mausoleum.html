Maus is no-gate, no-summon, no-recall, no-teleport
Warning: Easy to enter; can be very difficult to leave!

D - demon familiar (Ulrician Belt, Flail of Pestilance)
E - The entrance into the undead barracks
F - Foyer. You can summon from here
J - Juju Zombies (keys to their doors)
K - Kharzandra (cloak of wizardry, Shadow Dagger (Wielded=good), Libram of Gainful Conjuration)
O - Outside the Mausoleum (no-magic)
P - Dark priest vampire (Unholy Symbol of Lloth, Flaming Sceptre of Chaos)
R - Rakshaka (Cloak of Death, Sleeves of Harrapia)
S - dark spectre 
V - Shadar Lunn, the vampire (Darkened Armor, Guardian Sword, Arkham Signet Ring)

Somewhere in the maze:
  Lord Siig (Flawless Silver Shield, silver longsword "Vampire Slayer")
  Hellhounds [aggro < ca 50, hidden, EQ-BURNER]
  The Ancient Dracolich [Bølle] (Ring of Fortitude, Claw of the Ancient Dracolich)
