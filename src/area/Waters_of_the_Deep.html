U - under the docks, visible from HamsahMutaz
S - An enormous sea serpent (Serpent-scale armor)
C1 - Silver chest (treasures)
T - Tailor sahuagin (a seashell dagger)
W - weaponsmith (a bone-tipped spear, a coral dagger)
H - A Common House
a - Air chamber
M - Muddy Intersection
A - Altar/ Shrine. You can recall from here.
I - An Intersection.
G - Captain of the guards (in the pouch he wears, there is a key to unlock the door from whirlpool to waters of the deep).
K - Sahuagin King (Cloak of the Deep, spear of sahuagin, fine coral necklace, talon of the Sahuagin King)
    sahuagin priestess (ring of coral, shell necklace, small stone dagger).
C2 - chest (bone bracelet, 15 gold)
