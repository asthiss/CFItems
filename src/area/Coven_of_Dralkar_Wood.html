It seems some horrible error on the part of a usually reliable and confidential 
messenger service has caused a letter to be sent to you by mistake. It reads: 

"My Liege, 

I have discovered a small pocket of the lawless, hidden in a small but deep 
forest north of South Sutherspring Road. Infiltration has gone smoothly. 
It seems they are lead by a witch, who has managed to ensorcel the entire 
forest, making it impossible to stumble upon their village. But I have 
discovered subtle markings that show a trail which leads to a wall of mist. 
Find the markings by examining everything carefully, even the holes within 
the trunks of the dead trees. Such is not possible without the light of day. 
Passing through the mist undoubtedly takes you into their home. From there, 
avoid directions which fill you with foreboding, and you should remain safe 
from her deluding enchantment. I am preparing directions through the forest 
and a map of their homes using the usual codes. 

Your Servant, 
Y "
