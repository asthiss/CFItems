    Sitting low to the ground, this well-muscled cat looks ready to pounce or
    run at a moment's notice.  Its fur is matted and slick, appearing to be
    covered with a sheen of sweat.  Sharp teeth protrude from its jaws which
    look powerful enough to deliver crushing bites.  A lazy waving of its
    tail and eyes that track you are all that warn you that it notices
    you and is preparing its response.
