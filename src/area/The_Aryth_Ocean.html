Notes

Not a logical map. Going west from a (>-~) will put you on the opposite side of the map (~-<), and vice versa.
If you don't want to think about it, then from the mouth of river by Hamsah, go 2e, 19s, and you're at the Coastal Plains.
The bottom half of the map gets weird, so use the letters as a guide.
