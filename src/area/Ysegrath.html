Maze1:
Into K'sra : 
Look for "Something seems different about the swamp in this direction.". The direction is north.
path:   sw nnw n   from the first room off the main trail (room 1). The first sw to room 6 can be replaced with nnw, see, nwe

Into K'sra from any room of Maze1:
Mindlessly spam nnw until you get there. Finding room 1 and following above route works as well.

Back to AshesOfNoWhere:   
eee, wse, swe or ssee   From the first room south of K'Sra Entrance (room 9). Last e is from room 1 to T.

Back to AshesOfNoWhere from any Maze1 room: 
-Look east for "You think you see a trail." If you see it, you are in room 1. Do this every time you enter a room.
-If you are not in room 1, go south twice. 
-If you still haven't reached room 1, you are either in room 4 or room 8. Go west once. If you aren't in room 1 by now, go north to room 1.
